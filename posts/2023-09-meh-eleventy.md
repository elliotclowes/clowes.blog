I'm using [Eleventy](https://www.11ty.dev/) as the static site generator for this blog. And I have to say I'm not _fully_ sold on it. It seems fairly simple, which is what I want. And customising it hasn't been too tricky. 

However the tool for showing the website locally before then building it is pretty rough. Buggy and slow and just behaves oddly.
