The first use of the English word “history” wasn’t until 1482. And “historian” not until about half a century on from that. In many ways, the concept of “history” is relatively new. 

“History” has spent most of its life closely intertwined with stories, myths, legends and religious stories (via “Making History” by Richard Cohen).
